Instructions

This is a game of rock-paper-scissor


  1. First choose rock button or scissor button or paper button.

  2. This will show the name of the button you select and the computer chosen button.

  3. And then, this will show the points you scored and computer scored in score board.

  4. Now, the result shows who won.

  5. Use refresh to start the game, play over again.
